Connection to PyTorch DataLoaders

Your Generator

def line_generator(): for batch in batches: yield batch for batch in line_generator(): process(batch)

PyTorch DataLoader

loader = DataLoader( dataset, batch_size=32 ) for batch in loader: model.train(batch)
