Key Takeaways

  • Duplicates inflate accuracy and cause data leakage
  • Drop critical missing fields, fill non-critical ones
  • Standardize formatting to prevent model confusion
  • Boolean indexing filters invalid data cleanly
  • Proper data types enable correct operations
  • 80/20 train/test split measures real generalization
  • Every cleaning step directly impacts model quality

Remember: Garbage in, garbage out
